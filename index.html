▶ $sortmarry Command iDrag tiles below into your preferred order, then generate $sortmarry to reorder your harem in Mudae to match.
▶ $ci Image Commands iFor each character where you picked a specific image (via the 🖼 gallery button on a tile), this builds the $ci command to set that image in Mudae. Click each button in order, pasting into Discord between clicks.
